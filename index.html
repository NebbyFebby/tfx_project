Team Fortress 3

Brought to you by no one

In 2007, Team Fortress 2 came out a part of the Orange Box and in the 10+ years since, it has stood as a timeless masterpiece to many, bringing a fresh, innovative take on the multiplayer FPS genre. Now let us transition into a new chapter in the Team Fortress franchise

Welcome to Team Fortress 3

New Engine, New Look

With the upgrade to Source 2, Team Fortress 3 will look and run like never before while maintaining that classic art style we all love. It also makes updating the game much smoother, meaning developing new content will be a much smoother process

New Weapons and Maps

After years of demand, our beloved mercenaries are all receiving new weapons and gear from Mann Co. All new weapons can be earned through new contracts that will be added for free. Hopefully, it will have been worth the wait. And of course, we'll be introducing new maps and bringing back fan favorites.

Map Builder

We are proud to announce an official in game map builder. You'll be able to make your own custom maps and even gamemodes all with the various amount of tools that will be at your disposal. You can even host custom servers to play them with other players. We will also be introducing the ability to use community-made weapons via the Steam Workshop.
